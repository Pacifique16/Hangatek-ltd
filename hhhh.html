About Us

Our Story

HANGATEK was founded in 2015 with a vision to democratize technology and make cutting-edge digital solutions accessible to businesses of all sizes. Our team of experts brings together decades of experience in software development, cybersecurity, and digital transformation.

We believe in the power of technology to solve complex business challenges and create opportunities for growth and innovation. Our mission is to empower our clients with tools and solutions that drive efficiency, enhance security, and create competitive advantages.

Today, we serve clients across various industries, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, delivering exceptional value through our innovative approach and commitment to excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What industries do you specialize in?

We have experience working with clients across various industries including finance, healthcare, retail, education, and technology. Our solutions are tailored to meet the specific needs of each industry.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a custom enterprise solution could take several months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. Our support includes troubleshooting, updates, and feature enhancements.

What is your approach to project management?

We follow agile methodologies, ensuring transparency and regular communication throughout the project. Clients are involved in every stage, from planning to deployment, with regular progress updates.

How do you ensure the security of our data?

Security is our top priority. We implement industry best practices including encryption, secure coding standards, regular security audits, and compliance with data protection regulations.
